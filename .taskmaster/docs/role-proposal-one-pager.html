Director of AI Architecture

Role Proposal - Executive Summary
Prepared by: David Hayes
Current Role: AI Enablement Lead
Date: December 2025

Mission Statement

Lead the design, governance, and enablement of AI architecture across Fourth, orchestrating the research, tooling, personnel, and cross-departmental collaboration needed to successfully deploy AI solutions that drive business value.

The Opportunity

  • Microsoft investing heavily in enterprise AI (Copilot, Azure AI Foundry, Agent Framework)
  • Fourth can gain competitive advantage through coordinated AI adoption
  • Internal departments have AI needs but lack technical architecture expertise
  • A central function can accelerate deployment while managing risk
  • Leverage existing Microsoft enterprise agreements strategically

Scope

  • All internal business functions (BizOps, Finance, HR, Sales, Marketing, Support, Legal)
  • Internal productivity AI & workflow automation
  • AI governance, standards, and oversight
  • Cross-departmental AI initiatives

Core Responsibilities

  • AI Reference Architecture & Technology Standards
  • AI Governance Council & Tier-based Approvals
  • Cross-Departmental AI Enablement
  • Microsoft AI Ecosystem Leadership
  • AI Operations & Platform Management
